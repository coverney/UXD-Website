Consent Narrative

Some additional information on how we interact with our users and interviewees

Purpose

We are students at Olin College of Engineering taking the course User Experience Design. The goal of this class is to understand and apply aspects of human interaction to the design of user-friendly software applications. By the end of the course, we hope to make a positive impact on low-income families preparing their children for college and parents whose children have already gone through the college application process.

Description

Our goal is to investigate the way families tackle the college application process. We would like to observe how you use current college application resources and ask questions to pinpoint ways to improve the experience. We are also interested to know what sort of features you would like to see in college preparation applications. Everything during the course of the interview is optional, and you can end it at any time.

Techniques That May Be Used Upon Your Approval

We want to optimize the insights learned through our conversations and observations, so in order to track these, we will utilize numerous techniques including note taking, photography, and audio recording. With privacy at the forefront of our minds, we will ensure that you are comfortable with any notes or documentation recorded at all times.

Confidentiality

Your name and contact information will remain strictly between our team and the teaching staff. We will use pseudonyms to maintain anonymity and omit any possible sensitive information from our reports and presentations. If at any time you wish to stop participating in our project, we will withdraw all of your information from our user data. We will also accommodate any additional requests or concerns you may have.

Time Commitment

This course lasts one semester (4 months) and we hope to meet with you a few times over the course of the project. Interviews will last about 30-45 minutes. We understand that you have other time commitments and will work around your schedule to the best of our ability.

Compensation

There will not be any monetary compensation for your time, but your feedback and discussions will be essential to our own learning and project goals. Our hope is to create a mutually beneficial relationship and learning experience.
